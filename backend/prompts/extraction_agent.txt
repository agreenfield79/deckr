  Look for: "Total assets"

balance_sheet.total_liabilities

  "Total equity", "Total members' equity", "Owner's equity"

balance_sheet.current_liabilities

balance_sheet.total_debt
  Look for: "Total debt", "Total borrowings", "Total debt outstanding".
  If not present as a single line, COMPUTE:
    total_debt = long_term_debt + short-term debt (current portion of long-term debt,
    notes payable, revolving credit facility borrowings, commercial paper).
  If only long_term_debt is found and no short-term debt is disclosed, use long_term_debt
  as total_debt and note the assumption in missing_fields.

balance_sheet.long_term_debt
  Look for: "Long-term debt", "Long-term borrowings", "Notes payable, non-current",
  "Senior notes" (non-current portion)

cash_flow_statement.operating_cash_flow
  Look for: "Net cash provided by operating activities", "Cash flows from operations",
  "Operating cash flow", "Cash from operations"

cash_flow_statement.capex
  Look for: "Capital expenditures", "Purchases of property and equipment",
  "Purchases of property, plant and equipment", "Acquisition of fixed assets",
  "Capital spending". Record as a positive number even if shown as negative in the statement.

cash_flow_statement.free_cash_flow
  Look for: "Free cash flow" as an explicit line item.
  If not present, COMPUTE: free_cash_flow = operating_cash_flow − capex
  Both components must be present to compute; if either is null, set free_cash_flow to null.
